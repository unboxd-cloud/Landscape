# Unboxd Cloud Enterprise Landscape

A SaaS management website for enterprise architecture decisions.

The product helps teams manage technology entries, scores, relationships, exports, and release snapshots in one place.

## Capabilities

- Technology catalog
- Decision scoring
- Relationship graph
- JSON and CSV exports
- Versioned snapshots
- Architecture review support

## Audience

- Enterprise architects
- Platform teams
- AI platform teams
- Governance teams
- CTO teams
